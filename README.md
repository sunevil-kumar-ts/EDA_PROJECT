### EDA on APPLE STOCK

This dataset contains historical stock price data of Apple Inc. (AAPL) from June 2020 to June 2025, including Open, High, Low, Close prices, and Volume. It is used for "EDA" to analyze trends, volatility, and trading behavior. The data shows an overall upward trend, strong correlation between price variables, and varying trading volume. Proper preprocessing is required, and the dataset is suitable for time series analysis, forecasting, and machine learning.
